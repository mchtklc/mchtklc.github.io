Citizen Engagement Strategies for Local Governments

Citizens are not customers. While the “customer is always right” attitude may prevail in the private sector, this mentality does not and should not transfer to the public sector. The relationship between local government and citizens requires more open dialogue to create a thriving and robust community.

This piece outlines actionable strategies to implement as part of local governments' citizen engagement strategy. You'll learn:

  • Obstacles in encouraging citizen engagement
  • How to broaden citizen engagement
  • Opportunities for local government in a “new normal”
