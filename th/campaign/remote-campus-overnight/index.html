6 ways schools can provide better support during remote learning

The uncertainty surrounding recent events has put nearly all schools, colleges, and universities on unsteady ground. Faced with a “new normal,” the education industry is adjusting to a different campus environment.

Read on to learn six ways schools can adjust their support strategy as they prepare to accommodate for remote learning. Learn how to:

  • Simplify communication with custom ticket forms
  • Easily onboard new employees
  • Seamlessly collaborate on tickets internally
  • Provide best-in-class support to students and faculty
