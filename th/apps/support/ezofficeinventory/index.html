EZOfficeInventory

EZOfficeInventory คือซอฟต์แวร์ติดตามสินทรัพย์ระบบคลาวด์ชั้นนำ

(40)
  • คำอธิบาย
  • วิธีการติดตั้ง

EZOfficeInventory is the leading Asset Tracking Software, enabling organizations across the world to better utilize their equipment, assets, and inventory. EZOfficeInventory enables organizations to increase productivity, reduce losses and increase their return on investment (ROI) on valuable assets.

Getting on to EZOfficeInventory is a breeze and you'll be up and running in minutes. We support team collaboration around asset reservations, checkouts, and audits. Our mobile app can be used to help field teams keep track of assets and to scan QRCode and Barcode based labels.

The Zendesk app of EZOfficeInventory is used to manage tickets and incidents regarding equipment. Typical uses are:

  • An employee filing a ticket regarding their broken computer that needs to be fixed. EZOfficeInventory manages asset related details while Zendesk manages the ticket workflow.
  • A person in the field requiring help on using a drill machine, and an agent in Zendesk following up with specific details about the assigned machine via the EZOfficeInventory app.
  • A customer requesting memory extension for their MacBook. The IT Helpdesk looking up details on the assigned device, based on the model, determining the type of upgrade required. The details and past work on the MacBook being visible through the Zendesk app for EZOfficeInventory.
  • Logging EZOfficeInventory services and maintenance events in Zendesk to be assigned to the maintenance team.
  • Agents looking up outstanding Zendesk tickets for an asset in EZOfficeInventory.

We love talking to our users. If you have any questions or feedback, reach out to us at support@ezofficeinventory.com
