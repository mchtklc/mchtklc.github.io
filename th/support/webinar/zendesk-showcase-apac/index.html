Zendesk Showcase APAC

Available On-Demand

เกี่ยวกับ

There’s no blueprint for doing business right now. But one thing is for sure: as each day brings new challenges and unknowns, customer interactions count more than ever.

Zendesk Showcase APAC brings together business leaders to help answer the big questions facing organisations today. You’ll learn how to maintain seamless experiences with a remote workforce, working towards a ‘new normal,’ and hear from brands that are thriving in the face of adversity.

Agenda-at-a-Glance

  • Welcome to Zendesk Showcase APAC
  • Responding, managing, and leading during a pandemic
  • Zendesk Support Suite
  • The New Normal: Business as (Un)usual

Detailed Agenda

Welcome to Zendesk Showcase APAC – While we can’t be with you in person, we can welcome you with open arms. We’ll set the virtual stage for the exciting event ahead.

Responding, managing, and leading during a pandemic – Join Riccardo Pasto from Forrester as he discusses the steps in responding to, and leading through a crisis; including setting up a framework for sound decision-making.

Zendesk Support Suite – A global pandemic has only made Zendesk more keen to help reduce friction between companies and customers. Hear what our Support Suite can do.

The New Normal: Business as (Un)usual – Hear from Tom Keiser about how Zendesk is helping employees, customers, and the business navigate from crisis through rehabilitation to planning for a ‘new normal.’
