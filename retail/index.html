Zendesk for retail

Make your shop pop

Creating great customer experiences in retail is tough. Keeping customers happy means making every interaction matter. With Zendesk, you can make sure your customers get the support they need—wherever they’re interacting with your brand. Because happy shoppers are repeat customers—and that’s what makes a good brand great.

The Zendesk for retail approach

Get up close and personal

Complete shopper profiles let you better understand every customer. With Zendesk, you can bring together data from all parts of your business—including inventory, billing, shopping history, and preferences. Because the more you know about your customers, the easier it is to deliver exactly what they want—and make sure they keep coming back.

Deliver more personalized experiences with Zendesk Sunshine

Always be available

Gone are the days of emailing or calling a store during working hours—shoppers now have access to brands around the clock, on everything from chat to Twitter. With Zendesk, you can offer seamless support and a completely branded experience on every channel. That way, you can make sure your shoppers are getting the help they need, when they need it—so they can get back to more important things—like shopping. You can even reach out proactively, providing the right details at the right time.

Check out the Zendesk Support Suite for seamless omnichannel support

Turn DIY into AI

If shoppers are happy, business is growing—and that’s a good thing. Keep them happy by answering their questions quickly and efficiently. With a unified knowledge base from Zendesk, shoppers can get help right where they already are. Use Answer Bot and other automations to answer commonly asked questions—and free up your agents for things that require a more human touch.

Scale your support with Zendesk Guide and Answer Bot

Turn your vendors into trusted partners

Your products don’t see the light of day without the help of an extended ecosystem of suppliers, distributors, and vendors. From design and development to distribution and analytics, it’s easy to manage all the moving parts—every step of the way.
